## Buybacks

### How do buybacks work?

Every month:
1. Total bot profits are calculated
2. 40% is allocated to buybacks
3. We market-buy $DECRYPT on the DEX
4. 100% of bought tokens are burned (sent to dead address)
5. Transaction receipts posted to dashboard

### Why 40%?

It's aggressive but sustainable. We allocate:
- 40% â€” Buybacks (token value)
- 20% â€” Treasury (operations)
- 20% â€” Team (compensation)
- 10% â€” Fuel (account scaling)
- 5% â€” Repairs (drawdown recovery)
- 5% â€” Insurance (emergency reserve)

40% means almost half of every profitable dollar supports the token.

### Why burn instead of hold?

Burning removes tokens from supply permanently. If we held them:
- Future sell pressure risk
- Treasury could dump on holders
- Less transparent

Burn = done. No trust required.

### What if there are no profits one month?

No profits = no buyback. Simple.

The dashboard will show the P&L. If bots underperform, you'll see it. We have reserves (Repairs, Insurance) to weather bad months, but buybacks only happen when there's profit to fund them.

### Can I verify buybacks?

Yes. Every buyback includes:
- Transaction hash
- Amount bought
- Burn receipt
- Time stamp

All on-chain, all on the dashboard, all public.
