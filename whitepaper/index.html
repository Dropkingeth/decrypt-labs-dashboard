02Problem Statement

The retail trading industry is plagued by fundamental challenges that prevent consistent profitability. Despite access to the same markets as institutions, retail traders face systemic disadvantages:

Emotional Trading

Human psychology is wired against successful trading. Fear, greed, and FOMO lead to poor decision-making. Studies show that over 80% of retail traders lose money, with emotional trading being the primary cause.

Inconsistent Execution

Even traders with winning strategies fail due to inconsistent execution. Manual trading requires constant screen time, and fatigue leads to missed opportunities or deviation from proven rules.

Lack of Edge

Institutional traders use sophisticated algorithms and strategies refined over decades. Retail traders typically lack access to these methodologies and the infrastructure to execute them properly.

  • 80%+ of retail traders lose money over their trading career
  • Average holding time for losing trades is 2x longer than winners
  • Fear-based exits cost traders 40% of potential profits
  • Only 3% of traders maintain consistent execution over 12+ months

06Bot NFT Mechanics

Each Bot NFT represents ownership of an automated trading instance. The extreme scarcity (20 max supply) ensures each holder receives meaningful allocation and personalized support.

Supply & Scarcity

  • Max Supply: 20 Bot NFTs (hard-capped in smart contract)
  • Mint Price: 300 $CIPHER per bot
  • Strategy Lock: Each NFT is bound to a selected strategy at mint
  • Transferable: Full ERC-721 compatibility, tradeable on any marketplace

Fuel System

Bots operate on a fuel-based model. Fuel credits are purchased with $CIPHER and consumed as the bot executes trades. This creates ongoing token utility and ensures sustainable operations.

  • Fuel is consumed per trade executed (not per signal generated)
  • Unused fuel rolls over indefinitely
  • Bulk fuel purchases receive volume discounts
  • Fuel balance is tracked on-chain for transparency

Strategy Selection

At mint, holders select one of four available strategies. Each strategy has different risk/reward profiles and is optimized for specific market conditions. Strategy selection is permanent and tied to the NFT, but NFTs can be traded to holders who prefer different approaches.

07Revenue Model

Decrypt Labs generates sustainable revenue through multiple streams, all designed to align our incentives with holder profitability.

Revenue Streams

Our performance fee structure ensures we only profit when holders profit. There are no management fees, no monthly subscriptions, and no charges during losing periods. This alignment is fundamental to our philosophy: we succeed together or not at all.
