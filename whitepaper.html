Fuel System

Fuel is the monthly $CIPHER payment required to keep your bot active in Cipher City. Without fuel, your bot powers down. Fuel covers the trading account subscription, server infrastructure, and bot maintenance. $CIPHER is the native currency of Cipher City ‚Äî used for all transactions including minting, fuel payments, and City purchases.
